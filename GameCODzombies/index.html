Handy tips for Call of Duty Zombies

These are some handy tips for playing CoD zombies from the games World at war, Black ops 1, 2, 3 and 4. Originally known as Nazi Zombies, it is a game mode was made byTreyarch that first appeared in Call of Duty: World at War. It became highly popular upon the game's release, and returned in its sequels Call of Duty: Black Ops, Call of Duty: Black Ops II, Call of Duty: Black Ops III and in Call of Duty: Black Ops 4.

4 players,8 on Grief mode, have to survive waves of attacking zombies, getting points for killing zombies and repairing barriers. These points can be used to buy guns from the wall, onlock area's of the map, buy perks and buy guns from the mystery box and activate traps. There is no limit to the number of rounds; the game ends if all players have been Killed by Zombies, or if the player completes the easter egg on certain maps. Zombies will become stronger, faster and spawn in higher numbers every round, forcing players to make tactical decisions about point spending and exploring through the map. Sometimes zombies will drop power-ups such as Max Ammo, Nuke, or Insta-Kill if they die, making the rounds easier.
